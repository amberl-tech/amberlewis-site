About Me

IT Graduate • Developer • Wellness‑Focused Creator

I'm Amber Lewis — an IT and Software Development graduate with hands‑on experience in software troubleshooting, network structuring, and end‑user support. I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Networks from Oakwood University and I'm currently completing an AAS in Computer Information Systems at Calhoun.

I’ve worked as a Software Developer Intern at Noetic Strategies, maintaining and troubleshooting applications, and as an IT Intern at Fairview Park Hospital, where I supported network cabling, PC repair, and VOIP systems.

My technical background includes Java, C++, Python, Linux, Windows, server administration, and cloud fundamentals. I enjoy building clean, intuitive digital experiences that solve real problems.

Right now, I’m developing Living Wellness — a CrossFit‑inspired fitness and wellness tracking application designed to help people stay consistent and in control of their routines.

My long‑term goal is to grow in software development and cloud technologies while creating tools that make everyday life feel simpler, clearer, and more supported.
